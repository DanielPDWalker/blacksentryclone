About the game

Battle Abyss is a persistant incremental game, where you choose an enemy from a list, you fight them and hopefully win.

- Power Crystals, looted from fight increase your damage and max hp.
- Gold, also looted, allow you to change your player name (randomly generated).
- Kill, loot, get more powerful, then kill more for more loot!
